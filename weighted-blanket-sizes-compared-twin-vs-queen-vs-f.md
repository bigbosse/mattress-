## Twin Size Weighted Blankets

A Twin size weighted blanket typically measures approximately 48 inches by 72 inches. This size is specifically designed for distinct demographics: children, teenagers, or smaller adults sleeping on a Twin or Twin XL mattress.

Because the surface area is smaller, the weight is concentrated, providing intense deep touch pressure stimulation. ## Full Size Weighted Blankets

The Full size (often labeled as a Full/Queen hybrid depending on the manufacturer) generally measures around 54 inches by 72 inches or slightly larger. This size is a middle ground in the discussion of **Weighted Blanket Sizes Compared: Twin vs Queen vs Full**.

A Full size option is ideal for a single adult who requires more coverage than a Twin offers but sleeps on a Full or Double bed. It provides enough width to allow for turning over without the blanket sliding off the side of the mattress. It is rarely recommended for couples, as the dimensions are not sufficient to cover two people comfortably.

## Queen Size Weighted Blankets

The Queen size is the most popular option for adults, typically measuring 60 inches by 80 inches. These dimensions align perfectly with the top surface of a standard Queen mattress.

When determining the best option among **Weighted Blanket Sizes Compared: Twin vs Queen vs Full**, the Queen size offers the most versatility. It is spacious enough for a single adult who moves frequently during sleep and can occasionally accommodate couples, provided the total weight of the blanket is sufficient for two people.

## Summary of Dimensions

To make an informed decision, consider the standard measurements:

*   **Twin:** ~48" x 72" (Best for single sleepers, children, and narrow beds)
*   **Full:** ~54" x 72" (Best for single adults on full beds)
*   **Queen:** ~60" x 80" (Best for single adults on queen/king beds or couples)

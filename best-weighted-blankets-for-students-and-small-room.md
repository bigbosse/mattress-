## The Benefits of Deep Touch Pressure for Students

Sleep deprivation is a common issue in academic environments. Weighted blankets utilize Deep Touch Pressure (DTP) stimulation, which mimics the feeling of being held or hugged. This sensation encourages the body to release serotonin and melatonin while reducing cortisol levels. For students facing late-night study sessions and early morning classes, this physiological regulation helps calm the nervous system, allowing for faster and deeper sleep.

## Space-Saving Considerations

When living in a dorm or a small apartment, every square inch counts. Unlike bulky comforters that can overwhelm a small bed, weighted blankets are designed to fit the top of the mattress precisely. For a twin XL dorm bed, a blanket sized specifically for individual use prevents overhang, which acts as a counterweight and pulls the blanket off during the night.

## Key Features to Look For

To select the **best weighted blankets for students and small rooms**, several factors beyond just weight must be considered:

*   **Washability:** Accidents happen, and dorm laundry facilities are not always convenient. A weighted blanket with a removable, machine-washable cover is essential for hygiene and ease of care.
*   **Breathability:** Small rooms can fluctuate in temperature or become stuffy. Blankets made from bamboo or cool-to-the-touch cotton prevent overheating, ensuring comfort regardless of the season.
*   **Durability:** High-quality stitching (such as box stitching) ensures that the glass beads or pellets inside remain evenly distributed and do not leak.
